Jerk Chicken

Jamaican jerk chicken

A speciality of Jamaica,loved by
locals and visitors alike this chicken
is very spicy.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1 tablespoon spice powder
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon allspice

Directions

  1. place all the Ingredients in a blender and puree
  2. pour out all into a large bowl
  3. add the chicken.Ensure it is well coated
  4. refrigerate overnight
  5. bake over medium hot fire
